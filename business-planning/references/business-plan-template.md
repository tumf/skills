# Business Plan Template

Use this template when the user asks for a practical business plan that can be reviewed internally.
Adapt section depth based on stage and uncertainty.

## 1. Executive summary
- What business are we building?
- For whom?
- Why now?
- What is the recommended direction?

## 2. Planning context
- Planning objective
- Current stage
- Time horizon
- Primary reader
- Expected decision after reading
- Key assumptions
- Main constraints

## 3. Selected frameworks and why
For each framework:
- Name
- Why it is included
- What decision it informs

## 4. Market and strategic analysis
Possible subsections:
- PEST summary
- 3C analysis
- SWOT summary
- 5 Forces summary
- STP decision

Only include what was actually used.

## 5. Business model
Possible subsections:
- Lean Canvas summary
- Business Model Canvas summary
- Revenue logic
- Cost logic
- Channel logic

## 6. Product and customer scope
- Primary target customer
- Core customer problem
- Value proposition
- MVP or offer definition
- What is explicitly out of scope for now

## 7. Competitive edge
- Current advantages
- Assumed unfair advantage or moat
- What must be built to defend the position

## 8. Economics and metrics
- Revenue model
- What is sourced vs benchmarked vs estimated
- Driver model summary
- Low / base / high scenario outputs
- Unit economics assumptions
- KGI
- Leading KPI
- Risks in the numbers
- Highest-value research needed to improve confidence

## 9. Growth path
- Near-term plan
- Medium-term growth options
- Ansoff-style comparison if relevant

## 10. Validation and execution plan
- Next 30 days
- Next 90 days
- Key experiments
- Required decisions
- Dependencies and blockers

## 11. Risks and open questions
- Strategic risks
- Market risks
- Execution risks
- Unknowns requiring research

## 12. Recommendation
- Recommended path
- Why it is rational
- Why the primary reader should approve or support it now
- What must be true to continue investing

## 13. Reader optimization notes
- Primary reader's likely objection
- Evidence or argument needed to overcome it
- Sections to emphasize more strongly
- Sections to compress or move later

## Compression guidance

If the user wants a shorter output, compress sections 4 to 11 into:
- Situation
- Strategy
- Economics
- Validation plan
- Risks
